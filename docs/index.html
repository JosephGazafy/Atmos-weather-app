Privacy Policy for Atmos Core

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

Atmos Core is a sovereign security utility. We prioritize your privacy above all else.

1. Data Collection

Atmos Core does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, biometric signatures, or kinetic logs to external servers.

2. Biometric Data

All biometric verification (fingerprint/face) is handled locally by the Android BiometricPrompt API. Atmos Core never sees or stores your raw biometric data.

3. Local Permissions

Permissions for sensors and audio are used solely for the Kinetic Kill-Switch and Acoustic Shielding features. These processes occur entirely on-device.

4. Contact

For inquiries, contact the Sovereign-Operator via the project repository.
